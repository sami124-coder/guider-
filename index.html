STAGE 01

What do you need before arrival?

Complete these steps before travelling. Keep digital and printed copies of every important document.

✈
1

Check your admission documents

Confirm the registration date in your Admission Notice. Your personal notice and current university instructions always take priority.

2

Prepare your entry documents

Bring your passport, valid student visa, admission documents, and several recent blue-background ID photos. Keep copies of your passport information page and visa.

3

Plan your money and payments

Prepare enough RMB for transport, food, daily necessities, and initial housing expenses. Tuition depends on your program and scholarship.

Undergraduate¥30,000/yearMaster's¥35,000/yearDoctoral¥42,000/yearInsurance¥800/year
4

Choose housing before booking your flight

University housing applications generally begin in mid-July, while rooms are handed over from the beginning of September. If you arrive before the dormitory opens, you will need to stay in a hotel temporarily.

5

Plan your journey to Shenzhen

Save the campus address in English and Chinese, confirm your arrival time, and arrange airport or railway transport. Coordinate your arrival with your accommodation opening date.

STAGE 02

University information

Follow this order when you arrive and complete your university registration.

H
1

Complete university registration

Go to the location and time stated in your Admission Notice. Bring your original passport, visa, Admission Notice, JW201/JW202, and requested photos.

Official registration guide ↗
2

Activate your HIT account

Your unified identity account is used for student systems and tuition payment. Then activate your student email, Wi-Fi, mobile SIM, aTrust remote access, and campus card.

Student IDEmailWi-FiSIM cardaTrustCampus card
3

Pay the required fees

If your scholarship does not cover all tuition, pay the required amount by the end of registration day through the HITSZ Tuition Management System. Accommodation and other fees are handled separately.

Official fee instructions ↗
4

Complete the medical examination

Keep the original Physical Examination Record and a photocopy. It is required for your first residence-permit application.

5

Apply for your residence permit

X1 visa holders must apply within 30 days of entering China. Prepare the application form, medical record, temporary residence registration, photo receipt, passport copies, JW202, interview form, and university guarantee letter.

Processing normally takes about 10–15 working days. The official fee is ¥400 for up to 360 days.

STAGE 04

Study information

Set up your academic life and understand the rules that apply throughout your program.

◎
1

Read your academic guide

Review program requirements, the academic calendar, course registration, assessment, grades, attendance, and student-status rules. Undergraduate and graduate regulations differ.

2

Register for courses

Use your activated student account, follow your school’s course-selection instructions, and contact your academic office if required courses are missing.

3

Know the essential rules

Read policies covering exams, academic integrity, disciplinary action, dormitory conduct, insurance, scholarships, immigration, grade records, and Chinese-language exemptions.
